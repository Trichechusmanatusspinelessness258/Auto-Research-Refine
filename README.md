## 📋 What is Auto-Research-Refine?

Auto-Research-Refine helps users turn unclear research ideas into clear, step-by-step research plans. It breaks down the stage where you have a rough research question but do not yet have a detailed method. The software guides you through creating an executable and reviewable research proposal. After that, it helps you build a detailed experiment plan that supports your paper fully.

This tool combines three parts:

- **research-refine**: Polishes your fuzzy ideas into clear research points and a focused method.
- **experiment-plan**: Turns your stable method into a detailed plan for all needed experiments, covering main results, key tests, and resource planning.
- **research-refine-pipeline**: Links the first two steps into one smooth process to finalize your method and experiment roadmap.

## 🛠️ How Auto-Research-Refine Works

The tool helps you step by step.

### 1. Refining Your Research Idea

It turns your broad, fuzzy questions into clear problem points. It suggests a method focused on your main claim and keeps your plan concise and technically sound.

### 2. Planning Your Experiments

Once your method is set, it helps create a detailed list of experiments. This covers all types of tests, including main results and key checks for your claims.

### 3. Linking Both Steps

This pipeline combines the refinement and experimental planning in one smooth workflow.
